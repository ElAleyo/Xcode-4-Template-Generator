Info

The Xcode 4 template generator, is a simple script written in Python and it's meant to aid in the rather obscure process of creating Xcode 4 templates. As you may already be aware, Xcode 4 templates are very different from Xcode 3 templates, so taking a look at Boreal's Kiss A minimal project template for Xcode 4 is recommended. In that article you will find a lot of information about what is currently known about the new Xcode Template System.
